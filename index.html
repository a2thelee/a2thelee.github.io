My Origin Story

Hello, world! Life takes us to many unexpected places and in my case, I'm no different. The thought of becoming a software engineer had never crossed my mind for most of my life and my biggest regret is that I hadn't thought of it sooner. In my former life, I taught English to overseas students of all ages which came with its own challenges and rewards every single day. I am extremely fortunate that writing applications from scratch and seeing progress has also brought its own immensely satisfying challenges and rewards. What I love about coding is that I can be creative in how I approach any problems that need solving and I am in my element when implenenting a new feature piece by piece.
